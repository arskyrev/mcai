Japanese-inspired village area in Minecraft, featuring four main structures:

Central House:

Approximately 7 blocks wide, 5 blocks deep, and 4 blocks tall
Walls made of smooth sandstone or white concrete
Dark oak log pillars at each corner
Overhanging roof made of dark prismarine blocks, extending 2 blocks on all sides with upturned corners
Central wooden door on the front face
Two 1x2 windows on either side of the door, made of orange stained glass panes
Large lanterns hanging from the roof corners, made with gold blocks and glass panes


Side House (partially visible):

Similar in style to the central house
Visible corner with dark oak pillar and prismarine roof
At least one window visible, matching the central house


Background Building:

Larger structure with a distinctive pink roof, possibly made of wool or terracotta
Multiple stories visible
Traditional Japanese architecture style, possibly a shrine or temple
Details are less clear due to distance, but it appears to have a more complex structure than the other buildings


Wooden Archway/Torii Gate:

Made primarily of dark oak wood
Two vertical pillars supporting a horizontal beam
Lanterns hanging from the horizontal beam



Common elements and surroundings:

All buildings feature overhanging roofs in the pagoda style
A patterned pathway leading between buildings, made of a mix of cobblestone, stone, and sand blocks
Lush landscaping with tall grass, flowers (including white daisies and yellow dandelions), and custom trees
